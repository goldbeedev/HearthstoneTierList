About

Murloc Paladin is an aggressive deck that gains tempo through murloc synergy and cards like Call to Arms. This deck continues to be considered the best aggro deck alongside "dude" paladin, that spams Silver Hand Knights and buffs for synergy. After the Patches/Raza nerf, it made sense that this deck rose to the top of the aggro meta, Murlocs have always been powerful together. This deck has few weaknesses, and with the various token buffs, a real chance vs other control/anti aggro decks currently being run.

Weak Against

  • Control Warlock
  • Control Dragon Priest
  • Big Spell Mage
  • Cubelock

Strong against

  • Tempo Mage
  • Spell Hunter
  • Jade Druid
  • Miracle Rogue

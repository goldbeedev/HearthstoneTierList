About

With the recent cubelock nerf Taunt Druid sees a window of opportunity. Taunt Druid with Hadronox has made a strong comeback since the inception of Witchwood, as the expansion brought a new Druid card "Witching Hour" which allows players to resummon Hadronox after it has died. The deck never saw much play before this card, because players would just silence or polymorph the Hadronox. Even if it was played and destroyed, after killing all the resummoned taunts it left the player out of steam. This deck has powerful swing turns, and the ability to use Carnivorous Cube on the resummoned Hadronox makes it feel like an uphill battle quite often. Couple powerful minions, with seemingly infinite heal and you have a deck with a small window of weakness. The strategy is fairly straightforward: ramp up your mana early, play taunt minions to control the board, bring back said taunt minions multiple times with Hadronox combos.

Weak Against

Strong against

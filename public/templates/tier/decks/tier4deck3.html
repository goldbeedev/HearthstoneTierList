About

Spiteful Druid is another deck that many are unsure of its true position within the meta, the nerf to Spiteful Summoner has definitely had an effect on the overall frequency of the deck around the ladder. It appears with the rise of Taunt Druid and other wide board decks, Spiteful Druid falls down the meta standings as a deck that needs more refinement and testing. The deck wins games by playing Spiteful Summoner, which summons a 10 cost minion with it if you still have Ultimate Infestation inside your deck.

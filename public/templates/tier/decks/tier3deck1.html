About

Miracle Rogue has been around since the beginning of Hearthstone, and has had a few moments in the spotlight. Currently the deck remains strong, but is typically played as a counter to local meta states. If you find yourself facing a lot of Taunt Druid or Token Druid decks Miracle Rogue is the way to go. The deck wins games through the draw engine card "Gadgetzan Auctioneer" and combo turns that gain tempo and board presence.

Weak Against

  • Odd Paladin
  • Big Spell Mage
  • Odd Rogue

Strong against

  • Cubelock
  • Taunt Druid
  • Control Priest
  • Shudderwock Shaman

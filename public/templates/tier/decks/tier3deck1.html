About

Quest Rogue makes a comeback during the early Witchwood meta. The deck has a higher skillcap than most would think, and thus the more experienced players are able to pilot the deck to high legend ranks. This deck is punishing all of the top tier slower/combo decks around. Obviously the rush decks like Paladin punish this archetype more often than not. A lot of people are finding success running Vicious Scalehide, as it can provide an easy trade versus a small Paladin minion as well as that little bit of healing.

Weak Against

  • Even Paladin
  • Murloc Paladin
  • Odd Paladin
  • Odd Rogue

Strong against

  • Cubelock
  • Big Spell Mage
  • Control Priest
  • Spiteful Druid

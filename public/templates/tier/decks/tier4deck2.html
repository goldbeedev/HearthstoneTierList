About

Rush Warrior is a new archetype, and many that play it would think it would fare higher on this list. This is definitely one of the new decks to keep on eye on, as more people play it and lists become more refined, especially if some of the more powerful control variants slow down. This deck does a lot better than the other tempo decks on top of the meta versus the all-powerful paladin class, as the deck focuses on value trades to hold onto the board to snowball hero damage over the coming turns. Without a doubt this is the strongest deck versus other "board" type decks, aggro, tempo and mid-range alike.

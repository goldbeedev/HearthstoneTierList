About

Odd Rogue is another powerful aggro deck brought on by the introduction of Baku the Mooneater which upgrades your hero power at the beginning of the game if your deck consists of only odd cost cards. Many individuals have been able to reach top ladder positions grinding out this fast face deck, as the 2 damage 2 durability weapon from your hero power often gives just enough of a push for a quick win. The key to this deck is knowing when to stop fighting for board, and often goes face over trading unless it has to.

Weak Against

  • Cubelock
  • Big Spell Mage
  • Control Priest

Strong against

  • Spiteful Druid
  • Quest Rogue
  • Miracle Rogue

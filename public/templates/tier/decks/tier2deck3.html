About

Cubelock has been pushed down to tier 2. It appears the nerfs have successfully dethroned the juggernaut deck that has been terrorizing the meta for months now. With dark pact healing for 4 and possessed lackey costing 6 mana, Cubelock has far less room for error. Being that its combos are so powerful, and it packs a lot of easy board clear tools, it still remains a competitive deck to beat.

Weak Against

  • Big Spell Mage
  • Odd Rogue

Strong against

  • Even Shaman

About

Aggro Murloc Paladin was a strong deck before Witchwood, and it remains that way. The deck loses a couple murlocs, one which seemed vital - Vilefin Inquisitor, however, it also gained a strong neutral minion - Nightmare Amalgam. This new 3-4 minion is actually ALL different minion types into one, which makes it a flexible, solid body. Murloc Paladin is all about snowballing a board state, and of course if you lose your board, refill options like CTA and lost in the jungle provide what seem to be endless minion waves.

About

Control Priest is still a playable deck in the meta, but drops down the list with the rise of Druid. The various Druid decks provide so much heal and board refill, it makes it hard for Priest to get the mindblast combo into play. This deck Archetype was also seeing so much success pre-nerf due to the favored matchups with the two strongest tier 1 decks: Cubelock and Even Paladin, both which are seeing much less gameplay.

Weak Against

  • Taunt Druid
  • Token Druid
  • Miracle Rogue
  • Shudderwock Shaman

Strong against

  • Odd Paladin
  • Cubelock
  • Even Shaman

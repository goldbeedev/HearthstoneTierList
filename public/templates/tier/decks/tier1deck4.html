About

Even Warlock joins tier 1 as a new meta deck since the post expansion nerf. The deck uses a 1 cost hero power with all even cards thanks to Gen Greymane. This makes the deck feel almost "rampy" as you get to always draw a card on turn 1. Evenlock feels similar to an old school handlock build, collecting cards and then playing powerful threats that gain tempo off of card holding synergy like Mountain Giant. The deck feels like it has few weaknesses and a lot of strengths in the current meta. The featured list was created by Hearthstone Pro - Monsanto and hit rank 1 legend shortly after the nerf took place.

Weak Against

  • Big Spell Mage

Strong against

About

Tempo mage remains strong with the expansion, the only class this deck doesnt want to face is Paladin, however, it did get a couple of new tools in the way of Cinderstorm and Vex Crow. Vex Crow gives tempo mage the ability to do something it never has before - create tempo on board while using cheap spells. This makes games versus decks that go wide winnable, but it is still an uphill battle from the beginning, as Cinderstorm involves RNG and the Vex Crow doesnt get much value until around turn 6 or 7.

Weak Against

  • Even Paladin
  • Murloc Paladin
  • Odd Paladin
  • Spiteful Druid

Strong against

  • Cubelock
  • Big Spell Mage
  • Control Priest
  • Quest Rogue

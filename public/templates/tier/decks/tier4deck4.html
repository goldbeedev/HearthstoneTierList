About

Tempo Keleseth Rogue rounds out the bottom of the competitive tier list. It takes the last spot as it still has a really solid gameplan, unfortunately it just doesnt do the same thing the other top tempo/midrange decks are doing. It also has worse matchups versus the top tier control decks than the other tempo/midrange decks do. Basically if you are playing a midrangey tempo deck you want to stick to something around spiteful summoner or mage.

Weak Against

  • Cubelock
  • Big Spell Mage
  • Control Priest
  • Taunt Druid

Strong against

  • Miracle Rogue
  • Quest Rogue
  • Mid Range Shaman

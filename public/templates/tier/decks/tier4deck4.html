About

Tempo Mage had a great run before the nerfs, punishing decks like Cubelock and Control Priest. The decks current standing is unknown, but it should be mentioned as a meta deck, as it still has great matchups vs. a few of the other decks in the meta. The decline of Cubelock and Control Priest alone weakens the overall pool of matchups for this deck. You want to be playing this deck if you are facing other various combo decks, or current meta combo decks like Shudderwock Shaman or Miracle Rogue. It will be interesting to see if the meta shapes in a way that allows for another great Tempo Mage deck to surface. The deck struggles too much versus the other top meta decks to be considered at a strong position.

Weak Against

  • Spell Hunter

Strong against

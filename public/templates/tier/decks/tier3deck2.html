About

Malygos Druid makes a comeback as a tier 3 deck with the post expansion nerfs. Like Miracle Rogue, the deck works well depending on your local meta, punishing slower decks like Taunt Druid and Cubelock. The deck is able to do a considerable amount of damage by playing twig of the world tree, and then comboing it with malygos and an entire refill of your mana. The deck can burst down an opponent or easily destroy powerful threats with this combo. Several decklists claiming to have hit rank 1 have been floating around.

Weak Against

  • Odd Paladin
  • Odd Rogue
  • Even Shaman
  • Tempo Mage

Strong against

  • Cubelock
  • Big Spell Mage
  • Taunt Druid

About

Taunt Druid makes a comeback with Witchwood and makes Hadronox a relevant juggernaut. One of the reasons for this is the introduction of Witching Hour, which summons a random beast that died this game, this makes the deck get a ton of value with multiple taunt armies reviving over and over again. To further this strategy, the deck boasts a ton of armor gain and the infamous Carnivorous Cube for even more value with Hadronox.

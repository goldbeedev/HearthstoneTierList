About

Big Spell Mage remains in tier 2, but may wind up taking a spot in tier 1 before we know it. This deck uses powerful board clears and sustain cards like Frost Lich Jaina to defeat it's opponents through attrition. The deck is also capable of winning by playing minions on curve, or with powerful tempo swings through Alexstraza and Dragoncaller Alaina.

Weak Against

  • Tempo mage
  • Miracle Rogue
  • Shudderwock Shaman

Strong against

  • Odd Paladin
  • Cubelock
  • Even Shaman
  • Taunt Druid

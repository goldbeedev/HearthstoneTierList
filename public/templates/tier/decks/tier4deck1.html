About

Odd face Hunter is the new rush them down Hunter build. Originally this deck appeared to be meta-breaking, but once the meta started settling and decks became more refined it started to fall off a bit. It's still a strong contender, but often falls short versus the decks in the first two tiers. It is a deck that seems to either blow someone out of the water quickly or just struggle the entire game. The deck lacks draw and refill, so in theory Paladin and rogue just do it better.

Weak Against

About

Shudderwock Shaman is a combo deck introduced in the Witchwood expansion that makes use of multiple battlecries played throughout the game for a powerful combo turn with Shudderwock. The combo is capable of OTKing your opponent, but typically it takes multiple turns (which is easy to do once complete since every time you play Shudderwock, you heal, do damage to the opponent and add more 1 cost Shudderwocks to your hand. This deck is very fun, and can be strong based on your local meta, but should be played by only the most experienced players for ladder climbing.

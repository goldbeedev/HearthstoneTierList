About

Control Mindplast Priest earns a spot on the meta board, as it has very good matchups against pally, and it fares pretty well against other control variants in the meta. This deck was created before Witchwood, and gains a couple new tools - Divine Hymm and Scaleworm, both with promote longevity and sustain.

  • Tempo Mage

Strong against

  • Murloc Paladin
  • Spiteful Druid
  • Odd Paladin

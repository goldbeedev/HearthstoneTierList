About

Even Shaman joins the spotlight post Even Paladin nerf. The deck was being tested with the release of the even and odd deck types, but never made a huge impact on the meta with the Even Paladins running around everywhere. Several top 100 lists are around the interweb and this deck should show some promising results as the meta continues to refine. The deck makes strong tempo swings with the use of a 1 cost hero power, elemental cards, and the deathknight Hagatha the Witch

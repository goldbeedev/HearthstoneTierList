About

Tier 4 decks are decks that are being played in the current meta, but are not having much success vs the vast majority of playable meta decks. You should play these decks if you are highly experienced with the specific archetype, or if you want to play for fun, to learn a new deck playstyle. It is possible for these decks to regain higher positions as the new meta settles.

Decks to beat: Shudderwock Shaman, Spiteful Druid

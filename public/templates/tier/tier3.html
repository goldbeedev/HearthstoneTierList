About

Tier 3 consists of strong decks in the meta. Typically, climbing with these decks requires a more experienced player, as the room for error drastically decreases here. These decks are typically played based on your "local meta." If you are coming across a lot of decks within your ranking area that these decks hard counter, it may be time to try one.

Decks to beat: Miracle Rogue, Malygos Druid
